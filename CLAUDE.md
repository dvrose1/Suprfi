# SuprFi - Claude Context

## Project Overview
SuprFi is an embedded consumer financing platform for home service businesses. Technicians offer "Pay Later" options to homeowners directly from their CRM.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- React 18, TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- Prisma, PostgreSQL (Supabase)
- Clerk (auth), Twilio (SMS), Plaid (banking)

---

# Design Context

## Users
- **Now:** Investors evaluating SuprFi for funding (desktop, due diligence context)
- **Later:** Home service business owners and merchants exploring financing options
- **Job to be done:** Quickly assess credibility, understand the value prop, take next step (waitlist/contact)

## Brand Personality
**Premium, Sophisticated, Tech-forward**
- Confident without being aggressive
- Modern without being trendy
- Professional without being cold

## Emotional Goal
**Calm and reassurance** - Financing doesn't have to be stressful. The interface should feel like a trusted advisor, not a pushy salesperson.

## Aesthetic Direction

### References
Stripe, Plaid, Mercury (clean fintech)
- Generous whitespace
- Clear typography hierarchy
- Subtle, purposeful animations
- Muted color palette with strategic accent use
- No gradients, no glowing effects, no AI slop

### Anti-references
- Generic SaaS templates
- AI-generated designs with gradient text
- Glassmorphism
- Dark mode with neon accents
- Emoji in professional copy
- Em-dashes everywhere

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** - Every element should have a clear purpose
2. **Confidence through restraint** - Premium brands don't shout
3. **Trust through consistency** - Predictable patterns build confidence
4. **Mobile-first professionalism** - Merchants are in the field
5. **No AI slop** - Avoid em-dashes, gradient text, emoji, generic hero layouts
